Our Story

We bring homes to life

Bauhaus Designs is Bangalore's curated home for premium European furniture — one Indiranagar showroom carrying three design houses, chosen for material honesty and craft that outlives trends.

Most furniture in India is designed to be sold, not lived with. We started Bauhaus Designs to offer Bangalore the other kind: solid-hardwood frames, feather-wrapped cushions, full-grain leather, American walnut and oak — pieces made by houses that have been refining a single craft for decades.

Our floor at 100 Feet Road, Indiranagar carries three signatures: ALTO, contemporary luxury in walnut, brass and marble; Commune, Scandinavian-calm solid wood from Singapore; and Modulax, Belgian modular sofas and recliners crafted since 1957.

Everything is priced with GST and Bangalore delivery included, and in-stock pieces ship immediately. No showroom games — just furniture worth keeping.
