Bangalore's home for premium furniture

Searching for premium furniture in Bangalore usually ends in one of two places: mass-produced showrooms or long import waits. Bauhaus Designs is the third option — a curated luxury furniture showroom in Indiranagar stocking European-designed pieces you can see, touch and take home.

Our floor carries luxury sofas and sectionals in fabric, bouclé and full-grain leather; motorised recliner sofas engineered in Belgium; solid wood dining tables in American walnut and oak with marble and ceramic tops; designer beds with storage including motorised options; and accent chairs, coffee tables and consoles that finish a room.

Every piece is built on solid-hardwood frames with high-resilience foam, feather-filled cushions and premium upholstery — from Napa leather to woven rattan cane. Prices include GST and delivery within Bangalore, and in-stock furniture ships immediately to homes across the city — Indiranagar, Koramangala, Whitefield, HSR Layout, Sadashivanagar and beyond.

Good to know

Where can I buy premium furniture in Bangalore?

Visit the Bauhaus Designs showroom at No. 372, 100 Feet Road, HAL 2nd Stage, Indiranagar — one of Bangalore's leading destinations for luxury sofas, recliners, solid wood dining sets, designer beds and accent furniture.

Which furniture brands does Bauhaus Designs carry?

Three signature European lines: ALTO (contemporary luxury in walnut, brass and marble), Commune (Scandinavian-inspired solid wood living) and Modulax (Belgian modular sofas, sofa beds and recliners, crafted since 1957).

Do you deliver across Bangalore?

Yes — prices are inclusive of GST and transportation within Bangalore, and in-stock pieces are available for immediate delivery.

What materials go into the furniture?

Solid American oak and walnut, tropical hardwood frames, Napa and Padova leather, travertine and marble tops, natural rattan cane, serpentine-spring seats and high-resilience foam with feather-filled cushions.
