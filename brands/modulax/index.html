MODULAX

"Our furniture is not just made; it's crafted with love, shaped by history and designed for memories."

— The Modulax family, Bornem

Modulax has been making sofas in Bornem, Belgium since 1957 — a family, a tradition, and a brand that stands for more than furniture.

Modular sectionals that grow with your home. Sofa beds engineered for everyday sleeping, not occasional guests. Recliners with quiet motorised movement and hand-cut leather.

Every frame is built for decades: memories etched into the grain of the wood, laughter echoing off the tables, silent peaceful moments nestled into the comfort of the sofas.
