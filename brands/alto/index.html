ALTO

"I started from the idea to defy the norm, to do the unexpected, to create pieces that would have personality and a story to tell."

— Julian Koh, Chief Design Officer

ALTO by Commune is a global contemporary lifestyle brand creating uncommonly elegant objects — furniture that transforms living spaces through innovative design, premium materials and refined craftsmanship.

The Hotel Paradiso collection channels sun-soaked Mediterranean coastlines and the European glamour of the 60s and 70s: ribbed lines and fluted panels reminiscent of Italian architecture, travertine and green marble, walnut veneer and woven brass.

From bar units and record consoles to dining tables and beds, every ALTO piece merges traditional techniques with modern aesthetics — controlled through every step of the making process.
