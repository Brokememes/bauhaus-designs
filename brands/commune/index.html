COMMUNE

"We seek comfort in things that only nature can provide — a quiet solace, a neutral oasis."

— The Commune philosophy

Commune is a lifestyle furniture brand from Singapore built on clean lines, honest solid wood and a neutral, calming palette — Scandinavian sensibility with Asian craftsmanship.

The Rituals collection is designed around the daily coming-together of family and friends: smart storage, multi-functional pieces, soft-closing mechanisms, and LED-lit sideboards that glow through pleated fabric like lanterns.

American white oak frames, oak veneer panels, tropical hardwood rails — practical pieces that are as beautiful as they are lived-in.

Experience COMMUNE in Bangalore

The full COMMUNE range is on display at Bauhaus Designs, Indiranagar.
